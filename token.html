Symbolism

Why "SUTRA"?

A sutra is a thread—something that connects wisdom across time. In Buddhist tradition, sutras are teachings that preserve and transmit understanding.

The name reflects the project's connection to the Sutra and the Noble 8 AI music project—and the broader thesis that alignment works better through integrated values than through external constraints.

Part of a Larger System

SUTRA token connects several threads of alignment research:

  • -OneZeroEight.ai — Testing whether economic incentives can align AI behavior
  • -sutra.team — Persona architecture consulting (internal/phenomenological alignment)
  • -Sutra and the Noble 8 — 40+ songs demonstrating sustained AI persona identity
