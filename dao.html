The Concept

A decentralized organization for directing alignment research. Token holders vote on which questions to investigate, fund bounties for research, and collectively steer the direction of work on AI alignment and economics.

This is still in the concept stage. We're thinking through the governance mechanisms, the relationship to the SUTRA token, and how to structure research bounties effectively.
